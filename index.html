Who we are

TravelApp is a free and inspirational global travel search site comparing cheap flights, hotels and car rental deals. We give travelers an overview of available travel options without adding any extra fees. We don’t sell flight tickets, hotel rooms or car rental deals – we show you the available prices and let you choose the offer you prefer. One click and you’re taken directly to your chosen site where the booking is completed.
